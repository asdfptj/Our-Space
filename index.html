Galaxies and galaxies collide

Galaxies and galaxy collisions are one of the largest structures in the universe, a phenomenon in which different galaxies approach and collide. The Mystery of the Black Hole

A black hole is one of the most amazing phenomena in the universe, a place where gravity is so infinitely strong that not even light can escape. The website visually shows the phenomenon of black holes and introduces users to the mysterious world of black holes through intuitive explanations of gravitational distortion and theory.
